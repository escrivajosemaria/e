Membership Value

What the Collective
gives you

I
Guided Health Talks
Regular online sessions led by practitioners and experts across healthcare, public health, and humanitarian work — curated for depth, not just breadth.
II
Mentorship Access
Career discussions and mentorship pathways connecting early-career professionals and students with experienced practitioners willing to guide and share.
III
Volunteer Engagement
Opportunities to contribute to humanitarian and community health initiatives, bringing your skills and energy to places where they are genuinely needed.
IV
Innovation Dialogues
Structured conversations around health systems, technology, and creative solutions to public health challenges in East Africa and beyond.
V
Research & Learning
Access to reading circles, discussion on current evidence, and exposure to ongoing research in public health and development relevant to the region.
VI
Diverse Network
A community of students, professionals, educators, and leaders who bring varied backgrounds — making every session and exchange genuinely cross-disciplinary.
